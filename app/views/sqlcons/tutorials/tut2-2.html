Ch2-2: Inner Join cont.

We can also join multiple tables at a time, by adding another join clause. Since we are using a "dimensional" model for our database, course_fctsessentially links all the other tables together Lets say we want to see students associated with their courses information. "select fname, lname, title from course_fcts a join students b on (a.student_id = b.id) join courses c on (c.id = a.course_id)"
